About Our Heart Rate Monitoring System

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare by providing accessible, non-invasive vital sign monitoring solutions that improve patient outcomes and comfort while reducing healthcare costs.

We believe that advanced technology should be leveraged to make healthcare more accessible, convenient, and effective for everyone.

How It Works

Our system uses sophisticated computer vision and signal processing techniques to measure heart rate remotely:

1
Video Capture

High-resolution video of the face is captured in real-time.

2
Region Analysis

The system identifies and analyzes specific regions of the face.

3
Signal Processing

Advanced algorithms extract the subtle color changes related to blood flow.

4
Heart Rate Calculation

The system calculates heart rate based on detected pulse signals.

Key Benefits

  • Non-invasive: No physical contact required
  • Accurate: Clinical-grade measurement accuracy
  • Real-time: Instant feedback and monitoring
  • Accessible: Easy to use for anyone
  • Safe: No radiation or harmful emissions
